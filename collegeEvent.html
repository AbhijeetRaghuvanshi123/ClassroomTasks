College Sports Day Event

Our college is proud to announce the upcoming Sports Day event, a grand celebration of athletic spirit and excellence. This year, the event will be extra special as world-renowned Olympic players from across different sports will be joining us. Students will get a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to witness and be inspired by some of the greatest athletes who have made history on the global stage. It promises to be a day full of energy, competition, and unforgettable memories.
